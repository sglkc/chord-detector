What is this?

This page captures audio from your microphone, streams it to a Python backend over a single WebSocket, and runs the full chord-detection pipeline (librosa CQT, Superflux onset detection, then a small CNN trained on the OPUS chord dataset) on every chunk. The spectrogram and the latest predicted chord are shown back here in real time.

Open this page via http://localhost:8000 (or HTTPS in production) so the browser will grant microphone access. Play chords on a guitar, keyboard, or open a chord chart on another tab — any sustained tone should produce a prediction within a second.
